First Aid Kits

A first aid kit is an essential collection of medical supplies and equipment used to provide immediate care in case of injuries or emergencies. It typically includes items such as bandages, antiseptic wipes, adhesive tape, scissors, tweezers, gloves, and gauze pads. Having a well-stocked first aid kit at home, in the car, or at the workplace ensures that minor wounds, cuts, burns, or sprains can be treated promptly, reducing the risk of infection or complications.
